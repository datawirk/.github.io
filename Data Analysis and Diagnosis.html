Data Analysis & Diagnosis


Why Data Analysis?

Most companies have a primary product range that is the basis of their business and supports secondary products. These primary products each have an “Ideal Customer Profile” that is true for both business to consumer (B2C) and business to business (B2B). An Ideal Customer Profile considers the customers geographic, social, economic, time sensitivity along with many-many more activators which indicate the buying readiness of your customer or prospective customer.

Traditional sales forecasting systems would consider the purchasing history of a client for a minimum of 36 months, apply an algorithm to the data and forecast the likely hood of the next purchase. These forecasting systems are notoriously inaccurate.

Today we have moved to anticipating the next sale, instead of just looking in our rear-view mirror (traditional forecasting) we can look forward and backward and improve our forecasting accuracy.

With our client’s activity on the internet, social media, public profile, employee searches and enhancements from our master data base, we are able to predict with various degrees of certainty who your sales staff should call, when to call, what to say and how you can manage the relationship.
